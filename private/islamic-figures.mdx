## 🚀 Project Overview

This project aims to create a comprehensive online database showcasing over 1000 influential Islamic figures from the past century. The website will feature profiles of scholars, activists, fighters, and leaders who have made significant contributions to the Muslim world between 1924 and 2025. The platform will serve as a valuable resource for researchers, students, and history enthusiasts, providing detailed information, biographies, and their impact on Islamic culture and society.
